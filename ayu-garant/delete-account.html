How to Delete Your Account

To delete your Ayu Garant account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and login to your account.
  2. Go to the profile section.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

Once deleted, your personal data will be permanently removed from our systems in accordance with our privacy policy.

If you need help, you can contact us at: ayugarant@agi.kg
